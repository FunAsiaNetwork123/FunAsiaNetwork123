## 🏛️ &nbsp; About FunAsia Network

**FunAsia Network LLC**, the flagship entity of **Perfect Group Holding**, is a premier multi-industry conglomerate operating at the crossroads of entertainment, technology, and live experiences. From producing premium digital content to engineering cutting-edge platforms and curating world-class live events — we deliver excellence across every touchpoint.

Founded between 2016 and 2020, we have grown from a bold regional vision into a global powerhouse with active operations spanning the **Philippines**, the **Middle East**, and the **United States** — reaching audiences across **3 continents** and touching the lives of over **50 million people** worldwide.

> *"We don't just build companies — we build cultural landmarks."*
