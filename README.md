## 🌏 &nbsp;About Us

**FunAsia Network LLC — Perfect Group Holding** is a premier multi-industry conglomerate anchored in the heart of Asia-Pacific. We operate at the intersection of **entertainment**, **media**, **technology**, and **investment**, delivering world-class experiences and building lasting value for partners and communities across the region.

Our holding structure empowers a diverse portfolio of subsidiaries and strategic ventures — each operating with entrepreneurial agility, backed by the strength and vision of Perfect Group.
